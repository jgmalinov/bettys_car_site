Toyota RAV4 Hybrid

Toyota fully redesigned the RAV4 lineup, including the hybrid version, for 2019. For the first time, a more rugged version of the RAV4 Hybrid is coming for the 2023 model year. A few years after the redesigned RAV4 and RAV4 Hybrid arrived for 2019, a Woodland Edition arrives for 2023. Even if you don't find one of those special-edition models, all 2023 RAV4s get an updated Toyota Audio Multimedia. The RAV4 Hybrid competes with the Honda CR-V Hybrid, Ford Escape Hybrid, Kia Sportage Hybrid, and Hyundai Tucson Hybrid.

Toyota Highlander Hybrid

The Highlander Hybrid is a tried-and-true option for families looking for a three-row SUV with great fuel economy. Toyota introduced the latest, fourth-generation Highlander for the 2020 model year, and this year marks a mild midcycle update with a few new tech features. This version of the Highlander competes with other three-row hybrid SUVs including the Kia Sorento Hybrid and Ford Explorer Hybrid.

Toyota Corolla Cross

Toyota introduced the Corolla Cross for model year 2022 to slice the SUV space between the smaller C-HR and the larger RAV4. Like the Corolla sedan it shares its name with, the Corolla Cross is intended to provide affordable, reliable, straightforward transportation.A new hybrid model is the major change to the Corolla Cross lineup for 2023. Otherwise, given the Corolla Cross debuted only last year, changes are minor.

Toyota Camry

The 2023 Toyota Camry adds head-turning style this year, allowing the best bits of the TRD model to trickle down the midsize sedan's expansive trim lineup. Still one of the most popular four-doors available in the U.S. today, the Camry enters its sixth model year with a couple minor changes to keep it relevant against the new-for-2023 Honda Accord, the Hyundai Sonata, and the rest of the segment.

Toyota Tundra

After a too-long production run, the second-generation Toyota Tundra made way for the redesigned third-generation truck, which arrived for 2022. This full-size pickup is now equipped for the modern era, sporting an available hybrid powertrain and high-tech interior. Updates for the 2023 Tundra are limited to the addition of a new blacked-out SX appearance package.
